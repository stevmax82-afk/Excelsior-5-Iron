The Itinerary
Act 01  ·  Afternoon

First Stop

Five Iron Golf

Westin Mina Seyahi  ·  Dubai Marina

Three hours at one of Dubai's best venues, and everything is taken care of. Unlimited drinks. Unlimited canapés. And the simulators are all yours. Not a golfer? No problem at all. There's a proper bar, darts, and a pool table to keep things interesting. If you do fancy a swing, get your team together because there is a tournament running throughout the afternoon and the winning team walks away with a one month Five Iron membership, sponsored by Five Iron Golf.

Act 02  ·  Evening

Second Stop

Logs & Embers

Club Vista Mare  ·  Palm Jumeirah

As the sun drops over the Arabian Gulf, the evening shifts to this beloved beachside smokehouse. We've got an amazing happy hour menu lined up — think craft cocktails, cold drinks, and sharing plates at seriously good prices. Slow-smoked brisket, panoramic skyline views, all on the sand. This one is pay as you go, so order as much or as little as you like.
